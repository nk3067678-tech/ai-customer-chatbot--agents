# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

AI Customer Support Chatbot Agent

---

# 1. Product Overview

The AI Customer Support Chatbot Agent helps customers get fast and accurate answers using artificial intelligence and company knowledge.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Customers face delays in support because:

- Response time is slow
- Support teams handle many repetitive questions
- Customers cannot get support 24/7
- Finding correct information takes too much time

---

# 3. Product Goal

Build an AI-powered chatbot that provides accurate, fast, and helpful customer support while reducing support team workload.

---

# 4. Target Users

## Customer

Needs:
- Quick answers
- 24/7 support
- Easy conversation experience

## Support Agent

Needs:
- AI assistance
- Faster issue resolution
- Customer conversation history

## Business Team

Needs:
- Reduced support cost
- Better customer satisfaction

---

# 5. Core Features

## Feature 1: AI Chat Interface

Description:
Users can communicate with the AI chatbot through a simple chat interface.

---

## Feature 2: RAG Knowledge Search

Description:
AI retrieves information from company documents before generating answers.

---

## Feature 3: Conversation History

Description:
Store previous conversations to improve customer experience.

---

## Feature 4: Human Support Escalation

Description:
Transfer complex issues to human support agents.

---

# 6. User Stories

## Customer

As a customer,  
I want to ask questions to an AI chatbot,  
so that I can get quick support answers.

## Support Agent

As a support agent,  
I want AI assistance,  
so that I can solve issues faster.

---

# 7. Success Metrics

Measure:

- Response accuracy
- Customer satisfaction score
- Average response time
- Number of resolved queries
- Reduction in support workload

---

# 8. AI Requirements

- Language model integration
- Knowledge retrieval system (RAG)
- Response evaluation
- Safety and accuracy checks

---

# 9. Future Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP
- Basic chatbot
- FAQ answering

## Phase 2: AI Intelligence
- RAG system
- Document search

## Phase 3: Scale
- Analytics dashboard
- Multi-language support
- Advanced automation
