# AI Evaluation Plan - Customer Support Chatbot

## Purpose

Measure the quality, accuracy, and reliability of AI chatbot responses.

---

## Evaluation Metrics

### 1. Response Accuracy

Question:
Does AI provide the correct answer?

Measurement:
- Correct answers percentage
- Human review score

Target:
90%+ accurate responses

---

### 2. Response Time

Question:
How quickly does AI respond?

Measurement:
- Average response time in seconds

Target:
Response within 3 seconds

---

### 3. User Satisfaction

Question:
Are customers happy with AI responses?

Measurement:
- Customer feedback
- Rating score

Example:
👍 Helpful
👎 Not helpful

---

### 4. Knowledge Retrieval Quality (RAG)

Question:
Does AI find the correct information?

Measure:
- Relevant documents retrieved
- Correct context used

---

### 5. Safety and Reliability

Check:
- No incorrect information
- No harmful responses
- Clear fallback when AI does not know

---

## Testing Process

1. Create test questions
2. Compare AI answers with expected answers
3. Review incorrect responses
4. Improve knowledge base
5. Repeat evaluations.
