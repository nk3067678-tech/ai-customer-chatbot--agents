# Product Metrics Dashboard - AI Customer Support Chatbot

## Purpose

Track product performance, customer satisfaction, and AI quality after launch.

---

# 1. User Metrics

## Active Users

Measure:
- Number of customers using chatbot daily/monthly

Goal:
Increase chatbot adoption

---

## Conversation Volume

Measure:
- Total chatbot conversations
- Questions handled by AI

Goal:
Understand usage patterns

---

# 2. AI Performance Metrics

## Response Accuracy

Measure:
- Correct AI answers percentage

Target:
90%+ accuracy

---

## Response Time

Measure:
- Average time taken to generate response

Target:
Less than 3 seconds

---

## AI Resolution Rate

Measure:
- Percentage of customer issues solved without human support

---

# 3. Customer Experience Metrics

## Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)

Measure:
- Customer feedback after chat

Example:
👍 Helpful
👎 Not Helpful

---

## Escalation Rate

Measure:
- Number of conversations transferred to human agents

Goal:
Reduce unnecessary escalations

---

# 4. Business Metrics

## Support Cost Reduction

Measure:
- Reduction in support team workload

---

## Agent Productivity

Measure:
- Number of customer issues resolved per agent

---

# Monitoring Process

Weekly Review:

- AI accuracy
- User feedback
- Failed conversations
- Improvement opportunities
