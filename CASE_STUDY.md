# AI Customer Support Chatbot - Product Case Study

## 1. Overview

The AI Customer Support Chatbot Agent is an AI-powered solution designed to help customers get faster answers while reducing support team workload.

---

## 2. Problem

Customers experience:

- Slow support response times
- Limited availability of support
- Difficulty finding correct information

Support teams experience:

- Repetitive questions
- High workload
- Time-consuming manual responses

---

## 3. Product Solution

We built an AI chatbot that:

- Answers customer questions
- Uses company knowledge through RAG
- Maintains conversation history
- Escalates complex cases to human agents

---

## 4. Product Decisions

### Decision 1: Use RAG

Reason:
AI needs company-specific knowledge to provide accurate answers.

---

### Decision 2: Human Escalation

Reason:
Some customer problems require human support.

---

### Decision 3: Measure AI Quality

Reason:
AI products need continuous evaluation and improvement.

---

## 5. Success Metrics

Key metrics:

- Response accuracy
- Response time
- Customer satisfaction
- AI resolution rate
- Support workload reduction

---

## 6. Future Improvements

- Voice support
- Multiple languages
- Advanced analytics
- Personalized customer experience
